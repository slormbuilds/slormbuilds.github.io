ABOUT THE COMMUNITY AND THE GAME
This is a wiki for the game, The Slormancer, developed and published by Slormite Studios, featuring guides, builds, and more. Note that the wiki is still a work in progress.

The 2D Pixel-art Hack'n Slashy Action-RPG Dungeon Crawling game!
Fight hordes of enemies led by The Slormancer, an evil warlock from the past, in an absurd yet epic Action RPG! Featuring loads of shiny loot, tons of collectibles, countless hours of content, a frenetic gameplay and carefully crafted pixel art.

The Slormancer was published on Steam Early Access in April 2021, and has left Early Access for its 1.0 release the 13th of May 2025!

The official Discord is very active, and you should join it, especially if you have any question regarding the game.

Want to help build the wiki? Reach out to Legrems (Discord or Steam) I'd love to collaborate!
